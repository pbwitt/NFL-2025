Fair odds & implied probability

We map model probabilities \(p\) to American odds using \(\text{odds}(p) = \begin{cases}-100\,p/(1-p) & p\ge 0.5\\ 100\,(1-p)/p & p<0.5\end{cases}\). Implied probability from American price \(A\) is \(p=\frac{-A}{100-A}\) for minus prices and \(p=\frac{100}{A+100}\) for plus prices.
